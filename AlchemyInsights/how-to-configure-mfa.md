# <a name="configure-multi-factor-authentication-in-office-365"></a>Configure multi-factor Authentication en Office 365

Para configurar la MFA, vea [set up multi-factor Authentication for Office 365 users](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/admin/security-and-compliance/set-up-multi-factor-authentication).

Tenga en cuenta que debe ser un administrador global de Office 365 para habilitar y configurar la MFA.
